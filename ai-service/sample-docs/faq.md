# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I reset my password?

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
A password reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

## How do I change my email address?

Navigate to Account Settings and update your email address.
A verification email will be sent to confirm the change.

## How can I contact customer support?

Customer support can be reached through the Help Center or by emailing support@company.com.

## Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Billing section of your account settings.
